Cars

Director: John Lasseter

Produced by Pixar Animation Studios for Walt Disney Pictures.

Genre: Animated sports comedy

Year released: 2006
Fun Fact: Lightning McQueen's racing number, 95, is a tribute to the year 1995, when Pixar released it's first feature film, Toy Story

Cars is a 2006 American animated sports comedy film produced by Pixar Animation Studios and released by Walt Disney Pictures. The story follows a hotshot racecar named Lightning McQueen who gets stranded in a small town called Radiator Springs on his way to California for a big race. While in Radiator Springs, McQueen meets a variety of quirky characters, including Mater, Sally, and Doc Hudson,who help him learn important life lessons about friendship, humility, and the value of slowing down to appreciate the journey.
